  • Why I love Spider-Man

    Out of all the superheroes, Spider-Man just hits different. Sure, he's got the cool powers like shooting webs and swinging between buildings, but what really gets me is Peter Parker himself. He's just a regular guy trying to juggle school, work, and saving the city. He cracks jokes even in the toughest situations, and you gotta respect that. Plus, the whole "with great power comes great responsibility" thing? That's a motto we can all live by. Spidey just feels real, you know?
